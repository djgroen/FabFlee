The following older camps are converted to towns in this simulation, because there are no arrivals there over the whole multi-year simulation period.
- Mangaize
- Niamey
- Oudalan
- Tabareybarey

Soum is opened only after 1130 days, as there clearly are no entries before that.
Ayorou is opened only after 1090 days, as there clearly are no entries before that.

Start date is moved to 2018-01-01 because there are negative arrivals before that.
Simulation period is shortened to 1230 days, because there are no arrivals after that.
